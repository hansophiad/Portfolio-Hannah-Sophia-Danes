Get To Know Me

Hi, I'm Hannah - a UI/UX & Visual Designer currently based in Berlin, Germany.

Whilst diverse, my aesthetic approach tends to draw inspiration from nature - aiming to honour that distinct fundamental balance between organic randomness & functional order. Having been raised by an artist & an artisan in Zimbabwe, I had the good fortune to be surrounded by nature & art for as long as I can remember - and my fascination only grows with time. Living in Zimbabwe, especially as a creative, taught me to take a resourceful approach to every challenge, and to do so with a positive attitude. Traveling & experiencing new cultures - from New Zealand to Costa Rica and everywhere in between - really developed my appreciation for art & design by expanding my idea of beauty.

Ultimately, I want to positively impact people’s lives by creating meaningful products & brands that are not only visually appealing but also delightful to use/experience.
